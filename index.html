Ugly Foods

Supermarkets in Singapore generate an average of 2,500 tonnes of food waste on average annually, and most of it due to cosmetic filtering. Ugly Foods provides a B2B solution to this problem by connecting supermarkets with social organisations. More revenue for the supermarkets, cheaper ingredients for the organisations. Win-win.

Built with:
Ruby on Rails, React.js, HTML5, CSS3, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap
